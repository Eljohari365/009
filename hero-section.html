Engineering Division

Engineering Intelligence.
Digital Precision. Construction Excellence.

At Canadian Builders, our Engineering Division leverages the latest Building Information Modeling (BIM) technologies to transform ideas into fully coordinated, constructible, and intelligent digital models. By integrating design, engineering, planning, and cost management into a single workflow, we enhance collaboration, reduce project risks, and deliver greater certainty throughout the project lifecycle.

Our engineering team develops comprehensive BIM models up to LOD 500, ensuring every element is accurately coordinated for construction, commissioning, facility management, and long-term asset operation.

Using advanced 4D Construction Simulation and 5D Cost Modeling, we optimize project sequencing, improve productivity, control costs, and enable informed decision-making before construction begins.

1. Building Information Modeling (BIM)

Complete digital engineering solutions developed to the highest international BIM standards.

  • BIM Modeling (LOD 100 – LOD 500)
  • Architectural, Structural & MEP Coordination
  • Clash Detection & Model Validation
  • Shop Drawings & Fabrication Models
  • As-Built BIM Models (LOD 500)
  • Digital Twin & Facility Management Data

2. 5D Cost Management

Linking BIM models with quantities and cost data to improve financial control throughout the project lifecycle.

  • Automated Quantity Take-Off (QTO)
  • Cost Estimation
  • Budget Planning
  • Procurement Support
  • Value Engineering
  • Cost Control & Forecasting
